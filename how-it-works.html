How AeroBloom Works

AeroBloom simplifies indoor gardening with a step-by-step process:

  1. Setup: Assemble your AeroBloom unit and fill the water reservoir.
  2. Planting: Place your seeds or seedlings in the grow baskets.
  3. Nutrient Solution: Add the provided nutrient solution to the water.
  4. App Control: Connect your AeroBloom to the mobile app and customize settings.
  5. Automated Growth: AeroBloom's sensors and lights automatically manage the growing environment.
  6. Harvest: Enjoy fresh herbs and vegetables grown in your home!
